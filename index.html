The Watershed

A musical journey across rivers, storms, and spirit.

About Rainhouse

Rainhouse is the solo project of Dale Bass — a Bay Area-based poet and songwriter. The Watershed is a collection of songs exploring transformation, elemental forces, and the inner landscapes shaped by water, memory, and myth.

What is a Watershed?

wa·ter·shed (n.)
1. A boundary line dividing the drainage of waters of two streams or bodies of water.
2. A region drained by a river system.
3. A crucial turning point — a moment of transformation.
