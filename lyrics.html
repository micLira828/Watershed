Words from the waters — Part I & Part II

Part I

I am only made of flesh and blood.
She is the rain, the river and the floods.
She opens the way for me
and sees what no one else can see.

I have followed her into the sea,
where the ocean spirit waits for me.
the great spirit of the waters:
the ocean's only daughter,

who cries when the tides rise
and makes the rain fall.

when I travel far beneath,
I hear the sighs of those
who came before me.

I hold out my hands and rise
toward the spirit of the sea.

Soon, I will meet the ocean waves.
I will let them wash away
the sound of my name,
and the whisper that still remains.

I will shape my silence to the rain.
I will let it wash away the moss from my face
and the dust that still remains.

No, I am not okay.
the wind is calling out my name
toward the center of the pain
and the breaking waves.

and he is out there with me on the sea,
alone, with me.
